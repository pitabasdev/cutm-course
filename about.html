Centurion University of Technology and Management, located in Paralakhemundi, Odisha, is a leading institution dedicated to providing quality education in various fields of technology and management. Established with a vision to bridge the gap between academia and industry, Centurion University places a strong emphasis on practical, hands-on learning. With state-of-the-art facilities, a dynamic faculty, and a commitment to experiential education, the university has garnered a reputation for producing skilled and industry-ready graduates.


One of the distinctive features of Centurion University is its community-oriented approach. The institution actively engages with local communities, addressing societal needs through various initiatives. From skill development programs to community healthcare projects, Centurion University demonstrates a strong commitment to social responsibility. This holistic educational approach, combined with a focus on research and innovation, has positioned Centurion University as a hub for academic excellence and community development in the region.
